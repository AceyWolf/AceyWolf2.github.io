I am a game designer currently based in Paris.

About Me
Even before i could walk on my own, I had a controller between my hands, and had the chance to grew up alongslide video games. At first, I used to fantazised about what would be the perfect video game, then wonder how I could improve the ones I already love, and finally put some words about why I didn't like some of them. Studying Game design during my year abroad in Canada made me realize that I truly wanted to work in it.
On a more personal level, I mostly enjoy singleplayer games, from indie titles to AAA games. As long as the game has something unique to offer, whether it is a great story, a twist on the gameplay or a stunning art direction, I would be looking to play it.
Philosophy
I see video games as the most complete form of art, combining everything I love such as music, storytelling and interactivity. It is also the media that requires the most investment from people to fully enjoy it.
Nowadays, designing new experience for people to discovers can seems difficult as a lot has already been done, but I feel like we need to understand what has been made so far and modify or expand on those ideas.
Having more than 20 years of experience as a player gives me an asset to be able to offer some ideas to create new experiences.
Studies and career
I always wanted to work as soon as I can to be able to make some experience and ready myself as much as possible for the job market. That is why I did a two year diploma in Electronics and Electrical Engineering (DUT GEII) in Cachan, in an apprenticeship program at PSA Groupe, where I managed a test bench and designed a Python user interface. Once I had my diploma, I choose to spend a year abroad in Canada, to study at the Université du Quebec A Chicoutimi (UQAC) to discover different subjects, especially game design and game developpment. Back to France, I wanted to further my knowledges in game design and game development and chose ITESCIA to do a Master of science degree specialized in game development and producing. I also did this diploma in an apprenticeship program, this time working for Pacifica, Crédit Agricole's subsidiary insurance company, where I worked on updating an intranet website using Angular at first, then VueJS.
